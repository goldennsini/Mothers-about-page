About Pastor Peace

Pastor Peace Nsini Akpan has dedicated her life to education and spiritual leadership. With academic training in English & Linguistics and formal theological studies at Bible School, she founded Treasure Kids Cristain Academy to offer children an environment where academic excellence and moral formation go hand in hand.

Known for her compassionate counsel, rigorous standards, and warm presence, Pastor Peace blends scholarship with pastoral care — ensuring students grow in knowledge, discipline, and faith.

About Treasure Kids Cristain Academy

Treasure Kids Cristain Academy is a faith-centered primary school established to cultivate curiosity, integrity, and discipline. Our mission is to equip children academically while instilling values that last a lifetime.
